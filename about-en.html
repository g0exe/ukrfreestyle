About Us

Pioneers of Football Freestyle in Ukraine

Welcome to the UkrFreestyle team website! We’re among those who laid the foundation for football freestyle in Ukraine. It all started in 2008 in Kyiv when we, a group of ball-kicking enthusiasts, decided to turn our hobby into something bigger. That’s how our country's first professional football freestyle team was born.
Today, football freestyle is more than just tricks with a ball — it’s a true culture. Thanks to its accessibility and excitement, it has become a global movement that unites people worldwide. We’re proud to play a part in its rise, promoting a healthy, active lifestyle and inspiring creativity, especially among the younger generation.

What We Do

For us, football freestyle is both a sport and an art. We compete in national and international events, organize tournaments, and serve as judges. Beyond that, we put on unforgettable shows! With over 800 performances across Ukraine and in 20 countries, we’ve rocked sports festivals, stadium openings, kids’ parties, corporate events, TV shoots, masterclasses, and more. Every show is a chance to showcase the beauty of freestyle and energize the crowd.

Our Mission

This website is more than just our team’s calling card — it’s a platform to promote football freestyle in Ukraine. Here, you’ll find resources to help you dive into this sport. We aim to support the freestyle community and share knowledge with anyone interested.

Work With Us

Do you have an idea or proposal? We’re always open to collaboration! Let’s grow football freestyle together and create something awesome. Get in touch with us, and let’s make it happen!
