Training

Football Freestyle and Mastery Training

Dreaming of mastering football freestyle or individual football techniques? Want to learn cool tricks with the ball and confidently outplay opponents on the field? Then our football freestyle school is perfect for you!

Training for Everyone

We’re open to all enthusiasts, regardless of age, gender, or experience. The only requirement? A desire to train and grow. Regular training is the key to boosting your mastery, and persistence is your path to big achievements. Whether you’re starting from scratch or already consider yourself a pro, with us, you’ll uncover the secrets of ball control and take your technique to the next level.

What We Offer

Individual Training — A personalized approach for every student.
This is your chance to get one-on-one attention from a coach and focus on your football technique. Want to perfect your football freestyle tricks or improve ball control? We’ll create a tailored program based on your level and goals. Every session brings you closer to confident ball mastery and new personal records!

On-Site Masterclasses — Unique sessions for youth football teams.
Our on-site masterclasses allow young footballers to explore football freestyle and individual technique on your field! We visit youth teams to deliver dynamic sessions that build ball control, coordination, and a creative approach to the game. Kids learn awesome tricks and get a motivational boost from our coaches.

Join us and become part of a community where true mastery is born!

FAQ

How long does a training session or workshop last?
An individual training session lasts 1 hour. Training sessions or workshops for children’s teams range from 1 to 2.5 hours, depending on the number of participants, their ages, and the client’s preferences. We’re always ready to adapt the format to ensure the sessions are comfortable and effective for everyone.
What ages are your training sessions suitable for?
We focus on children aged 8 and up. We have experience training kids aged 6-7, but such sessions are arranged individually. Write or call us to discuss the details!
Is special equipment required to participate?
All you need is comfortable football shoes and your ball. We provide balls during training, but don’t forget about independent practice. It’s best to use your own ball to get used to it and progress faster.
How do I sign up for individual training?
Individual training is available by prior arrangement. You can also ask any questions by calling +38 (068) 593-12-46. We’ll help create a training plan tailored just for you.
How do I invite you to hold a workshop?
Call us at +38 (068) 593-12-46 or message us on Telegram, WhatsApp, or Viber. We’ll discuss the details to ensure the workshop fits perfectly into your event.
Where do your training sessions take place?
All training locations are in Kyiv. Currently, our mobility is somewhat limited, so the range of locations has slightly decreased. You can see our regular training spots on the map above. You can also call us to discuss alternative options.
How much does it cost to participate in training or workshops?
The cost depends on the session format (group, individual, or on-site), the number of participants, and the duration. To get current prices, contact us – we’d be happy to explain all the details!
Is prior experience required to participate?
No prior experience is needed! Our training is suitable for both beginners and those with some experience. We adapt the program to each child’s level so everyone can enjoy the process.
Is there a training schedule?
All training sessions and their weekly frequency are arranged individually. We’re flexible with scheduling to match your availability.
What if my child is afraid to try?
We welcome parents to stay nearby during sessions. From the very first training, we aim to engage participants, taking into account their unique traits and preferences. It all depends on their willingness to participate, so the final decision is up to the child. We’ll help create a comfortable atmosphere where everyone feels relaxed and confident.
